What We Treat

We help with more than you might think

Chiropractic care works by restoring proper function to the spine and nervous system — which affects every organ, tissue, and cell in your body. The range of conditions it can help with often surprises people.

Back Pain

One of the most common reasons people see a chiropractor. Whether it's acute lower back pain, chronic stiffness, or disc-related issues — we assess the underlying cause, not just the symptom.

Headaches & Migraines

Many headaches originate in the neck and upper spine. Chiropractic adjustments can reduce frequency and severity significantly for many patients.

Neck Pain

Whether from poor posture, a desk job, injury, or stress — neck pain responds very well to chiropractic care. We address both the pain and the cause.

Sciatica

Sciatica — pain that radiates from the lower back into the leg — is often caused by nerve compression. We work to identify and relieve the source of that pressure.

Sports Injuries

From weekend warriors to competitive athletes — we help you recover faster and perform better by keeping your spine and nervous system functioning at their best.

Pregnancy Care

Chiropractic care during pregnancy can help with back pain, pelvic discomfort, and nervous system function — supporting both mum and baby through every trimester.

Infant & Child Care

The birthing process and early childhood are full of physical stresses on the developing spine. Gentle chiropractic care from birth helps give children the best possible start.

Poor Posture

Modern life — desk work, devices, driving — creates postural patterns that strain the spine over time. We help correct these patterns before they become serious problems.

Sleep Issues

Poor sleep is often connected to nervous system imbalance. Many of our patients report significant improvements in sleep quality as their spinal health improves.

Stress & Fatigue

Chronic stress creates physical tension in the body. Chiropractic care helps regulate the nervous system — which can have a meaningful impact on how your body handles stress.

Hip & Knee Pain

Biomechanical issues in the spine and pelvis often contribute to pain in the hips, knees, and lower limbs. Addressing the source — not just the pain site — is key.

General Wellbeing

Many of our patients don't come in with a specific complaint — they come in because they want to function at their best. Proactive, preventive chiropractic care is at the heart of what we do.
