Questions

Common questions

Do I need a referral?

No — you can book directly with us at any time. No GP referral required.

Does chiropractic hurt?

Most patients find adjustments comfortable or even relieving. We always work within your comfort level and will explain what we're doing before we do it.

Is it covered by health insurance?

Many private health funds cover chiropractic care. Check your extras cover for details. We can provide you with a receipt to claim.

How long will my first visit take?

Allow around 45–60 minutes for your initial consultation. Follow-up visits are typically shorter.

Do I have to commit to ongoing care?

Absolutely not. The $29 consultation is standalone — no obligation. After your assessment, we'll present your options and let you decide what's right for you.
